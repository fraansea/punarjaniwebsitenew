Frequently asked
questions

Everything you need to know about the
product and billing.

What types of yoga classes do you offer?

We offer various yoga styles including Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, and Restorative yoga. Each class is designed to cater to different fitness levels and wellness goals, ensuring everyone can find the perfect practice for their needs.

Do I need any prior experience to join your fitness programs?

No prior experience is needed. Our programs are designed for all fitness levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our experienced instructors will guide you every step of the way.

How do I book a personalized coaching session?

You can book a session through our website using the "Book Now" button, or by calling our reception desk directly. We'll help you schedule a time that works best for your needs and connect you with the right specialist.

What should I bring to a yoga class?

Bring comfortable clothing that allows for movement, a water bottle to stay hydrated, and your own mat if you prefer. We also provide mats and props for those who need them.

Are your nutritional plans tailored to individual needs?

Yes, all our nutritional plans are customized based on your goals, dietary requirements, and lifestyle. Our nutrition experts work closely with you to create a sustainable plan that supports your wellness journey.

How often are wellness workshops held?

Wellness workshops are held monthly and cover various topics including mindfulness, stress relief, and holistic wellness. Check our events calendar for upcoming dates and registration details.
